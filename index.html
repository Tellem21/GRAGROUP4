GRA

since establishment

Since its establishment in 2009, the Ghana Revenue Authority (GRA) has been dedicated to efficiently managing and collecting taxes to support the nation's development. The GRA was founded with the mission to promote voluntary compliance and provide quality customer service, ensuring the effective mobilization of revenue. By integrating and streamlining tax administration, the GRA aims to maximize revenue collection while maintaining transparency and fostering a cooperative relationship with taxpayers.

In our effort to achieve our mission and objectives, we uphold a strong sense of integrity, treat taxpayers with fairness, and maintain mutually beneficial relationships with all stakeholders.

Our corporate social responsibility initiatives are focused on promoting positive actions through activities that benefit taxpayers, the environment, consumers, employees, communities, stakeholders, and all other members of the public who may also be considered stakeholders.

Therefore, we have corporate social responsibilities towards taxpayers, the media, the government, businesses, our clients, the general public, and the global community in which we operate.
